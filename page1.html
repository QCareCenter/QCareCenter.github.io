QCareCenter Initiative Goals 

  • Makeshift Covid Care Units are designed to be rapidly established and run for 3-6 Months 
  • Infrastructure Reuse - can be packed and moved to needed locations
  • It addresses patient need between Home and Hospital i.e Asymptomatic, Mild oxygen dependency (less than 5L/min) Patients.
  • Use of Remote technology  and Best Practices in care delivery to bring down need of actual Doctors in the unit
  • Not for profit but run with efficiency of private enterprise
  • Open Source the Blueprint to establish Rapid Covid Care Center
